# PPTX DESIGN GUIDELINES: CELLUPULP BD

## 1. Visual Theme: "Industrial Resilience"
- **Color Palette:**
  - Primary: `#0D9488` (Teal - represents sustainability and industrial precision)
  - Secondary: `#0891B2` (Cyan - represents water and ZLD tech)
  - Accent: `#10B981` (Emerald - represents green growth/jute)
  - Neutral: `#F8FAFC` (Off-white - backgrounds)
- **Typography:**
  - Headers: **Inter / Arial Black** (36pt+)
  - Body: **Calibri / Segoe UI** (14-16pt)
- **Layout Motif:**
  - Consistent 0.5" margins.
  - Teal accent bars on the left side of content slides.
  - High-quality 3D renders/icons for every slide.

## 2. Slide Structure
- **Hero Slides:** Dark background (Teal) with high-contrast white text.
- **Content Slides:** Light background with teal headers and data visualizations.
- **Data Slides:** Minimalist charts matching the brand palette.

## 3. Brand Assets
- Logo: `public/logo.png` (Top right corner on all slides)
- Icons: Custom teal circular icons for key pillars (Risk, Operations, Financials).
